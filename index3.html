The 8th SEAMS-UGM 2019 Conference

Deepening Mathematical Concepts for Wider Application through Multidisciplinary Research and Industries Collaborations

Following the commitment in mathematical activities to SEAMS (South East Asian Mathematical Society), once in every four years, Universitas Gadjah Mada in cooperation with SEAMS organize an International Conference on Mathematics and its Applications in Yogyakarta, Indonesia. The 8th SEAMS-UGM 2019 Conference will be conducted on July 29th – August 1st, 2019. The scientific program will include keynote lectures, invited lectures, contributed presentations, and mini-symposia on selected topics. The objectives of the conference are to provide a forum for researchers, lecturers, educators and students to exchange ideas, to communicate and discuss research findings and new advances in mathematics in the wide area of mathematics, statistics, actuarial science, and computer science (theoretical, applied, computational). This conference is also aimed to deepen mathematical concepts and widen its application through collaboration with industries. It is also hoped that the conference will enhance collaboration between researchers from the countries in the region and those from outside the country.
